How we work
Simple process, serious thinking.
No 50-slide decks. No chaos. Just a structured way to understand your world, design something appropriate, and quietly make it real.
Step 01
Listen

Understand your context, constraints, and what “success” actually looks like — from your side, not a template.

Step 02
Design

Sketch the architecture, flows, and outputs. We keep things small, modular, and realistic for your team to maintain.

Step 03
Build

Implement pipelines, dashboards, code, or automations. Ship in small slices, so you see progress early.

Step 04
Grow together

Adjust, extend, and support. Stay on as a quiet partner, or wrap up cleanly when the work is done.

About the company
Small on purpose. Serious by design.
JCB is being built slowly and intentionally — to stay human, to stay sharp, and to do work its founders are proud of, not just busy with.
JCB is headquartered in Houston, Texas and exists at the intersection of data, software, and human-centered systems.

The long-term vision is not to become a giant, anonymous consultancy. It’s to stay compact, trustworthy, and strong — a place where skilled people bring their whole mind to work and still have a life outside of it.

Our ideal clients are teams who care about clarity, honesty, and sustainable pace. People who want things done properly, without drama.
