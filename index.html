Process
A simple way of working.
Not a complicated methodology. Just a clear sequence so everyone knows where we are and what happens next.
Step 01
Listen

Understand your current reality: systems, constraints, and what “better” would actually look like from your side.

Step 02
Design

Shape a simple plan: architecture, flows, and outputs. Focused and realistic for your team, not idealized on paper.

Step 03
Build

Implement in small, visible pieces: pipelines, dashboards, automations, small tools — whatever serves the goal.

Step 04
Grow together

Adjust, maintain, and expand calmly. Either stay on as a quiet partner, or hand things over cleanly.

About
Small on purpose.
JCB is designed to stay compact, thoughtful, and personal — not to grow into a tower where nobody knows who is doing the work.
Jnyan Cit Bajrang LLC, operating as JCB TechHub, is based in Houston, Texas.

The goal is to be a dependable, clear-thinking partner for a small number of teams — rather than trying to “scale” at any cost.

JCB values people who are good at what they do, curious, and grounded. People who want to build things well, earn fairly, and use the rest of their energy on the parts of life that matter to them.

What the name holds:

Knowledge (Jnyan) – keep learning and working from solid understanding, not guesswork.

Consciousness (Cit) – stay aware of the humans, not just the systems or charts.

Strength (Bajrang) – be reliable in work, in communication, and in standing by the people who trust you.
